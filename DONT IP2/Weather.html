What is Accuweather?

Accuweather is an online platform thay helps users gain insights on weather in various countries across the globe. In simple terms, it's a weather tracker.

The Accuweather platform gathers weather data in various formats. Weather data can be organised according to forecasts, historical weather and various other formats according to the countries searched up. The platform is said to host forecast data for 3.5 million locations across the globe, provided in over 200 languages.

Data Gathering

Accuweather gathers data insights from a multitude of different sources that stem from a plethora of different organisations. Namely, Accuweather does get some of its data from the National Weather Service, and other existing weather organisations from across the globe. Thanks to this, Accuweather hosts a very extensive database of all collected weather data from these sources.

Expert Analysis

At Accuweather, the organisation hosts a very strong team of professional meteorologists, who constantly analyse and moniter weather patterns in order to provide accurate weather predictions. Their team, paired with their very own weather forecasting algorithms, ensure that the weather data that they provide is indeed as as accurate as possible.

Accurate Forecasting

Thanks to the combination of the previously mentioned points, Accuweather is able to produce its forecasts successfully. It currently employs over 170 different models in order to produce these accurate forecasts. Thanks to the work of various organisations across the globe, Accuweather is able to provide solid forecasts for a plethora of different locations.
